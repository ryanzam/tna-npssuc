Turku Nepal Association (TNA) was established in 2014 as a nonprofit organization with the aim of organizing cultural and social events to promote Nepal and Nepalese community residing in Turku, Finland.

TNA supports members of Nepali community in Turku and provides information about Nepal as a place to visit and do business in. In addition, the association raises funds for operations and development activities for the Nepali community as well as communities in Nepal.
